Military Services

Aircraft Seals By Trelleborg Sealing Solutions

IASA is authorized to resell Trelleborg products including kits for various military platforms. Trelleborg products are used in almost every major commercial and military aircraft program and are commonly specified by leading aircraft manufacturers and OEM customers as sealing solutions on a wide range of critical applications.

IASA Group is proud to team with Trelleborg Sealing Solutions to offer the most complete range of Trelleborg sealing solutions.  Trelleborg Sealing Solutions is the major international sealing provider, uniquely placed to offer dedicated seal design and development from their market-leading product and material portfolio.  Together with IASA we form a one-stop-shop providing the best in elastomer, thermoplastic, PTFE, and composite technologies for applications in aerospace, industrial and automotive industries.

Supporting valued customers around the globe.

Since the beginning, IASA Group has maintained a strict compliance with the ISO 9001:2008 standard throughout its operations. Our team is focused on meeting military and government needs across the globe, even the most pressing AOG and urgent deadlines, and providing a cost-effective solution for all our clients regardless of size and volume.

Additionally, the IASA Group offers consignment services for traceable surplus inventory from government, corporate, regional and international flight departments throughout the world.  Working through our partners we are capable of meeting your project or recurring needs for structural, electrical/ avionics systems and other engineering, design, certification and airworthiness needs!

Custom Logistical Kitting Solutions

Our team of dedicated professionals specialize in giving aging systems new life by providing kits and assemblies to maintain, overhaul and modify proven DOD equipment.  At IASA Group we have an array of kitting and supply chain management capabilities that our customers have come to rely on to help them overcome their most difficult supply chain barriers.

IASA is equipped with instant access to parts availability inventories worldwide, giving us the capability to locate even the hardest-to-find parts within your region or area of remote operations. IASA Group, has the experience, systems, partners and knowledge to locate, quote your requested parts and repairs FAST.

Aerospace Hardware, Consumables & Chemical Coatings
